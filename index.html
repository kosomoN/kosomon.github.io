Biography

I’m a Swedish-speaking Finn from Helsinki, working as Research Staff at Aalto University & Nvidia researching deep learning for medical imaging. Currently, I’m focusing on low-dose computed tomography (CT) reconstruction. I’m collaborating with Prof. Jaakko Lehtinen as well as researchers from Nvidia.

When I’m not researching, I design and build hand-crafted furniture. I especially enjoy mid-century modern design and Japanese joinery. Currently available for commissions.
